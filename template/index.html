Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)

Dosage: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours as needed (max 4g per day)

A pain reliever and fever reducer that works by blocking the production of certain natural substances that cause pain and fever.

More Information

Ibuprofen

Dosage: 200-400mg every 4-6 hours as needed (max 1200mg per day)

A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that reduces hormones that cause inflammation and pain in the body.
